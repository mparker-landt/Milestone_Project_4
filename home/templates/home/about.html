About The Stave

Welcome to The Stave, your destination for all things musical. Whether you're a seasoned musician or just starting your musical journey, we are here to support and inspire you every step of the way.

Established in 1991, The Stave was a simple musical instrument repair business operating out of the founders garage. Over the years we have grown into a fully fledged business selling our products worldwide and known for our commitment to quality and customer satisfaction.

We offer a diverse selection of musical instruments to suit every taste and skill level. Instruments of all musical groups are available for purchase as well as sizes for younger musicians.
