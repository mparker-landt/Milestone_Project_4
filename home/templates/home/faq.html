Frequently Asked Questions

What is your return policy?

Returns can be made within 42 Days of purchase. Refunds can only be issued to your original payment method. Shipping is non-refundable, except in cases where we have made a shipping error. We do not have an "exchange" process - simply return your item and place a new order for the alternate item.

How do I make a return?

If you have any questions or need assistance, please contact us through our Contact Us page. Whatever the problem, please let us know - our customer service staff will work with you until it's resolved.

Once I place my order, how soon will you ship it?

Your order will ship out within 2 Business Days.

How long will it take for my order to arrive?

Standard shipping time within the United Kingdom takes 5-10 business days. International orders usually take 3-5 weeks to arrive depending on destination and customs processing.

What shipping method do you use?

Orders are shipped via RoyalMail, UPS, DHL, FedEx or DPD.

What forms of payment do you accept?

We gladly accept Visa, MasterCard, Discover, and American Express.
